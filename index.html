What Thread does

Everyone has had that moment.
"Does this entry look right to you?"

Approving 50 journal entries on the last day of the quarter. A CFO asking why revenue came in at X when you billed Y. A C-suite that wants to understand their financials but does not know the rules of debits and credits. Thread shows the full P&L and balance sheet impact of any entry in one click — for everyone in the room, not just the accountant who booked it.
